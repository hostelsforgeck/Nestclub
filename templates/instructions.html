Hostel Types

Men's Hostel (MH)

Men's Hostels (MH) offer comfortable and affordable living accommodations exclusively for male students and working professionals. These hostels provide essential amenities such as furnished rooms, common areas, and security services to ensure a safe and conducive environment for residents.

Ladies Hostel (LH)

Ladies Hostels (LH) cater specifically to female students and working professionals, offering a secure and supportive living environment. These hostels are equipped with modern facilities, including furnished rooms, common spaces, and round-the-clock security to ensure the safety and well-being of residents.

Paying Guest - Men (PG-M)

Paying Guest accommodations for men (PG-M) provide an affordable and flexible living option for male students and professionals with home owners. Residents can enjoy furnished rooms, shared common areas, and basic amenities while experiencing a homely atmosphere.

Paying Guest - Ladies (PG-L)

Paying Guest accommodations for ladies (PG-L) offer a cost-effective and comfortable living solution for female students and professionals with home owners. These facilities provide furnished rooms, shared living spaces, and essential amenities in a safe and welcoming environment.

Full House - Men (FH-M)

Full House rentals for men (FH-M) are ideal for male students or professionals seeking private and spacious living arrangements. These houses come fully providing a convenient and independent living experience.

Full House - Ladies (FH-L)

Full House rentals for ladies (FH-L) offer a private and spacious living solution for female students or professionals. These houses are fully ensuring a convenient and independent living environment.
